Relationships in astrology are analyzed through several key areas of the chart: the 5th house (romance and dating), the 7th house (committed partnerships and marriage), the 8th house (deep intimacy and shared resources), and the planets Venus and Mars. Synastry (comparing two charts) and composite charts add further depth.

The 5th House governs romance, dating, pleasure, and creative self-expression in love. This house shows how you flirt, what attracts you initially, and the playful side of love. Leo and Sun energy here indicates warm, dramatic romance. Venus in the 5th house loves romance and creative dating. The 5th house also rules children and creative projects, which are often expressions of love.

The 7th House governs committed partnerships, marriage, business partnerships, and one-on-one relationships. This is the most important house for serious relationship astrology. The sign on the Descendant (7th house cusp) reveals what you seek in a partner. Libra on the Descendant seeks harmony and beauty in partnership. Aries on the Descendant seeks independence and excitement. Planets in the 7th house show qualities you project onto partners and need to develop within yourself.

Venus represents your love style, what you find beautiful, and how you express affection. Venus in Taurus loves stability and physical touch. Venus in Gemini needs intellectual stimulation and variety. Venus in Cancer is nurturing and seeks emotional security. Venus aspects to other planets modify how love expresses. Venus square Saturn can indicate difficulty with vulnerability or delayed relationship timing.

Mars represents passion, desire, and sexual chemistry. Mars in Aries is direct and passionate in pursuit. Mars in Scorpio is intense and deeply desiring. Mars aspects to Venus reveal the chemistry dynamic between partners. A harmonious Venus-Mars aspect indicates natural sexual chemistry, while a challenging one can indicate passion mixed with conflict.

Synastry is the comparison of two birth charts to assess relationship compatibility. Key synastry indicators include Venus-Mars connections (chemistry), Moon-Moon connections (emotional compatibility), Sun-Moon connections (core understanding), and Saturn-Venus connections (commitment potential). The houses of the other person's planets falling in your chart show where they impact your life.

The 8th House in relationship astrology governs deep intimacy, sexual bonding, shared resources, and psychological merging. Strong 8th house connections in synastry indicate fated, transformative relationships. Pluto contacting personal planets in synastry can indicate intense, karmic bonds that trigger deep healing or power dynamics.

Composite charts (the midpoint chart of two individuals) show the relationship itself as an entity. The composite Sun shows the relationship's core purpose, the composite Venus shows how the relationship expresses love, and the composite Mars shows how the relationship takes action and handles conflict.

Lunar aspects between charts are crucial for long-term compatibility. Harmonious Moon aspects indicate natural emotional understanding, while challenging Moon aspects require conscious work to understand each other's emotional needs. The 4th house in synastry shows home and family compatibility.
