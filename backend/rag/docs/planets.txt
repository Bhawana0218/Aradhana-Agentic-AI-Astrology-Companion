In astrology, the planets represent the fundamental drives and energies that shape our personality, desires, and life experiences. Each planet governs a different aspect of our being, from our core identity to our deepest subconscious patterns.

The Sun is the center of the chart, representing your core self, ego, vitality, and conscious identity. It shows the essence of who you are and the qualities you are developing throughout your life. Your Sun sign is what most people know as your zodiac sign. The Sun rules Leo and is exalted in Aries. It governs the heart, vitality, and the creative life force.

The Moon represents your emotional nature, instincts, habits, and subconscious mind. It reveals how you nurture yourself and others, your emotional needs, and your automatic responses. The Moon rules Cancer and is the fastest-moving celestial body in the chart, changing signs every 2-3 days. It governs the stomach, emotions, and the feminine receptive principle.

Mercury governs communication, thinking patterns, logic, and how you process and share information. It rules Gemini and Virgo and is never more than 28 degrees from the Sun. Mercury retrograde periods occur 3-4 times per year and are times when communication and technology may be disrupted, though they are excellent for reflection and revision.

Venus represents love, beauty, values, and what you find pleasurable. It shows how you express affection, what you find attractive, and your aesthetic sensibilities. Venus rules Taurus and Libra and governs relationships, money, art, and social harmony. Venus is never more than 48 degrees from the Sun.

Mars is the planet of action, desire, ambition, and physical energy. It shows how you assert yourself, your drive, and your approach to conflict and competition. Mars rules Aries and is the traditional ruler of Scorpio. Mars governs the muscles, adrenaline, and the masculine assertive principle.

Jupiter is the planet of expansion, growth, luck, wisdom, and abundance. It shows where you experience good fortune and where you are called to grow beyond your current boundaries. Jupiter rules Sagittarius and is the traditional ruler of Pisces. Jupiter takes about 12 years to orbit the Sun, spending roughly one year per sign.

Saturn represents discipline, responsibility, structure, karma, and life lessons. It shows where you face limitations and where you build lasting achievement through hard work. Saturn rules Capricorn and is the traditional ruler of Aquarius. Saturn takes about 29.5 years to complete its orbit, which is why the Saturn return around ages 29-30 is such a significant milestone.

Uranus governs innovation, rebellion, sudden changes, and awakening. It represents the urge to break free from convention and express your individuality. Uranus rules Aquarius and takes about 84 years to orbit the Sun, spending approximately 7 years in each sign.

Neptune rules dreams, intuition, spirituality, illusion, and transcendence. It governs the realm of imagination, psychic sensitivity, and the dissolution of boundaries. Neptune rules Pisces and takes about 165 years to orbit the Sun, spending about 14 years in each sign.

Pluto represents transformation, power, rebirth, and the deep unconscious. It governs the processes of death and regeneration, both literal and metaphorical. Pluto rules Scorpio and takes about 248 years to orbit the Sun, though its orbit is highly elliptical.

Each planet also has dignity in certain signs (domicile, exaltation, detriment, fall) which modifies its expression. A planet in its domicile is comfortable and expresses its nature freely. A planet in exaltation is honored and expresses its highest potential. A planet in detriment struggles to express its nature. A planet in fall is weakened or challenged.
