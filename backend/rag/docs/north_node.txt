The North Node and South Node (also called the Lunar Nodes) are not physical bodies but mathematical points where the Moon's orbit crosses the ecliptic. They form an axis that reveals your karmic path and soul purpose. The South Node represents your past life gifts and comfort zone, while the North Node represents your growth edge and life direction in this incarnation.

The North Node is what you are moving toward in this lifetime. It represents qualities you need to develop, experiences you need to embrace, and the direction of your soul's evolution. The North Node often feels uncomfortable at first because it asks you to grow beyond familiar patterns. The house and sign of the North Node show the area of life where you are called to expand and take risks.

The South Node represents innate talents, natural gifts, and patterns carried from past lives. While the South Node offers comfort and ease, staying in South Node energy can lead to stagnation and unfulfillment. The South Node shows what you tend to fall back on when stressed, but what you ultimately need to move beyond.

North Node in Aries / South Node in Libra: This soul is learning independence, assertiveness, and self-reliance. Past lives focused on partnership and pleasing others. The soul path is to develop healthy self-assertion and leadership.

North Node in Taurus / South Node in Scorpio: This soul is learning stability, self-worth, and material security. Past lives involved intense transformation and shared resources. The soul path is to build steady, tangible foundations.

North Node in Gemini / South Node in Sagittarius: This soul is learning curiosity, communication, and intellectual flexibility. Past lives focused on being a teacher or authority. The soul path is to embrace learning through connection and exchange.

North Node in Cancer / South Node in Capricorn: This soul is learning emotional vulnerability, nurturing, and home. Past lives emphasized career and public achievement. The soul path is to create inner and outer emotional security.

North Node in Leo / South Node in Aquarius: This soul is learning creative self-expression, joy, and authentic individuality. Past lives involved group consciousness and collective causes. The soul path is to shine authentically and lead from the heart.

North Node in Virgo / South Node in Pisces: This soul is learning practical service, health, and daily discipline. Past lives were dreamy and spiritually focused. The soul path is to bring spiritual insight into practical, earthly form.

North Node in Libra / South Node in Aries: This soul is learning partnership, diplomacy, and balance. Past lives focused on independence and self-assertion. The soul path is to relate harmoniously and create fair, balanced relationships.

North Node in Sagittarius / South Node in Gemini: This soul is learning higher truth, wisdom, and meaning. Past lives focused on information gathering and communication. The soul path is to seek deeper understanding and share wisdom.

North Node in Capricorn / South Node in Cancer: This soul is learning discipline, responsibility, and public achievement. Past lives focused on family and emotional security. The soul path is to create structure and authority in the outer world.

North Node in Aquarius / South Node in Leo: This soul is learning community, innovation, and collective vision. Past lives focused on personal creativity and leadership. The soul path is to contribute to the greater good through unique gifts.

North Node in Pisces / South Node in Virgo: This soul is learning faith, surrender, and spiritual connection. Past lives focused on analysis, service, and perfection. The soul path is to trust the unseen and embrace divine flow.
